Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Never click on suspicious links in emails or messages
2. Always check the URL before entering sensitive information
3. Do not share OTPs, passwords, or bank details with anyone
4. Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
5. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
6. Be cautious of urgent or threatening messages
7. Regularly update your apps and devices
8. Use trusted and secure websites (HTTPS)

UPI Safety Tips:
9. Never approve unknown payment requests
10. Do not scan random QR codes
